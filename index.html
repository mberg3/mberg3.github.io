When I'm not doing research, I enjoy reading, yoga, and hiking in the national parks.

Research

I study the circumgalactic medium of galaxies, which is the region of space between the intergalactic medium and the galaxy disk. This region houses large-scale gas flows that influence whether or not a galaxy will continue to form stars. I observe these gas flows using QSO absorption line spectroscopy to investigate the connection between the gas and the galaxy. Here is a list of my publications.
